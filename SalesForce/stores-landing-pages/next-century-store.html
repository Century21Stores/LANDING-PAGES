ABOUT THIS STORE

Next Century is a curated concept store hosting special designer collaborations, exclusive events and partnerships. It features a diverse mix of high-end and streetwear designers, in addition to rare vintage pieces and exclusive limited-edition items. The space will also showcase new collaborations and exclusive celebrity/influencer merchandise facilitated by fashion technology company CALA.
