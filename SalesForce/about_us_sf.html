About Us

OUR HISTORY

Century 21 Department Store opened its doors in Downtown Manhattan in 1961. It is now a NYC retail icon, known for designer brands, amazing prices. What started 6,000 sq. ft., 3-story store is now 220,000 sq. ft. and 7 floors of fashion, expanding with new locations across the country.

THE FIRST CENTURY

The owners name the company after a World's Fair exhibit: "Century 21, The World of Tomorrow" (a decade before the real estate firm).

1961

BROOKLYN CALLING

Based on demand, a second location opens in Bay Ridge—the hometown of co-founders Al and Sonny Gindi

1961

EASTWARD BOUND

Good things come to those who wait! C21 opens its 3rd stores in over 30 years in Long Island, exanding the company's reach with loyal NY fans.

1999

NEVER FORGET

The downtown flagship is heavily damaged on 9/11. The Gindi family is determined to rebuild and mantain its downtown roots. C21 reopens in the summer of 2002.

2001

WESTWARD TRAILS

Due to sucess in NY, Century crosses the bridge and sets up its first NJ shop in historic Morristown.

2002

GO JERSEY

"New York's Bet Kept Secret" comes to Bergen County, NJ as the anchor of the Bergen Town Center mall.

2006

KING OF QUEENS

C21 opens its 6th store in Rego Park, Queens. The motto at that time is "fashion worth fighting for." But with this opening, Time Out New York calls it "fashion worth travelling for."

2010

DIGITAL AGE

C21Stores.com launches, making designer brancs and amazing prices customers love available 24/7.

2011

MOVING ON UP

C21 celebrates 50 years in business with the opening of its Lincoln Square store, making this store number 7.

2011

GARDEN STATE

Customer continue to embrace C21 with a new location in the Jersey Gardens Mall, catering to tourist straight from Newwark Airport, who are ready to fill up their suitcases.

2013

HELLO PHILLY

The first PA store opens at Market & 8th Street in the heart of Center City, Philadelphia—C21's first venture outside NY and Nj.

2014

GREEN ACRES

This landmark 10th store is the star of the Green Acres Mall, taking on yet another prominent anchor position in a mall.

2015

BACK TO BROOKLYN

The City Point Stores opens in one of Brooklyn;s trendiest areas making the first full-line department store to open on Fulton street in more than 50 years.

2016

SUNSHINE STATE

Meet C21's first store in sunny Sawgrass Mills, Fl—an 85,000 sq. ft. anchor in the largest outlet destination in the U.S. It's the furthest C21 has expanded from its NYC roots.

2016

CROSS COUNTY

Marking its first location in Yonkers, New York, C21 expands its footprint to a newly renovated space in The Mall at Cross County

2017

WATCH US GROW

More exciting things are ahead. Stay Tuned!
