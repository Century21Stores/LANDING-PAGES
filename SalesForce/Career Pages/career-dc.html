LOGISTICS CAREERS AT CENTURY

Logistics supports the Century 21 supply chain by getting merchandise to our Stores and Guests. The Logistics team takes pride in making sure our Stores are well supplied with the highest quality products delivered quickly and efficiently. Our four Distribution Centers are constantly turning new product but most importantly, they are staffed by energetic and determined C21ers committed to delivering value and the goods…every single day.

“The reason it’s different here than working in other places---and believe me, I’ve been working here since age 16—are all the opportunities I’m getting here to learn things that will help me develop as a person in my profession. It’s an advantage of being in a smaller company. You don’t get bored.”

SECAUCUS: THE 6TH BOROUGH?

We like it in NJ and think that you will, too. In addition to our NY corporate offices, we have corporate employees across our four warehouses in Secaucus, NJ. Our ecommerce, marketing and creative teams sit in our EDC at 700 Secaucus Road, right next to our HDC. HR, IT, finance and other key positions are located across our other warehouses, the ADC at 1 Emerson Lane and the SDC at Enterprise Road.

Secaucus is a quick, 10-minutes-flat train ride from Penn Station in Manhattan on NJ Transit or a short bus trip in as little as 20 minutes from Port Authority. One reason to consider crossing the Hudson River for your next job? You will enjoy significant tax savings working in New Jersey vs. New York. And if you already live in Jersey, how perfect is that?

corporate shuttle

Our free corporate employee shuttle makes getting to work between NY and NJ even easier. It runs every few minutes during rush hour between the Secaucus train station and our four warehouses, the EDC, HDC, SDC and ADC.
