WHAT IS THE C21STATUS REWARDS PROGRAM?

The C21STATUS Rewards Program is designed to reward frequent guests of Century 21 Department Stores with C21STATUS Points that can be redeemed for Reward Dollars to use toward future purchases. C21STATUS Points are based on guest purchase activity within a rolling 12-month period based on program enrollment and first date of usage as a C21STATUS Rewards member.

When you join C21STATUS as a loyalty or credit card guest you will earn points on qualifying merchandise at any Century 21 Department Store location and online at C21Stores.com. Earn 500 points and receive a $10 reward to use on future purchases at Century 21 Department Stores. In addition, special offers and benefits are available by tier. To learn more, click here.

WHAT IS THE DIFFERENCE BETWEEN A C21STATUS RED LOYALTY GUEST VS. A C21STATUS CREDIT CARD GUEST?

C21STATUS Red Loyalty guests earn 1 point per $1 spent on qualifying merchandise. C21STATUS Credit Card guests earn points and rewards quicker when they are approved and use the C21STATUS Credit Card.

Premier credit card holders earn 2 points per $1 spent on qualifying merchandise when they use their C21STATUS Credit Card while Elite credit card holders earn 4 points per $1 spent on qualifying merchandise when they use their C21STATUS Credit Card. Spend $1,500 per year to qualify as an Elite cardholder. In addition, credit card holders have special benefits, including priority checkout in stores, special offers and events, and free shipping on online orders over $50.

Premier and Elite credit card guests can still earn 1 point per $1 spent if they do not pay with the C21STATUS Credit Card so long as they provide the email address associated with their C21STATUS Rewards account in-store or log in to their account online.

DO POINTS EXPIRE?

If you do not earn 500 points within a 12-month period based on your transaction history, then those points will begin to expire on a rolling basis. For example, 100 points earned in February this year will expire in February next year if you have not accrued a total of at least 500 points. The balance, minus the 100 points that expired, will remain.
