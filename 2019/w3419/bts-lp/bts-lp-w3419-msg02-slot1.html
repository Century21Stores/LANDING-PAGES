This season, we take a lifestyle approach to value and luxury, blending the designer brands and amazing prices our shoppers love with the current meme culture we all live. Inspired by the relatability of memes and the modern grind of city life, we draw on those classic New York moments to present luxury fashion in a way that’s elevated, yet playful. The campaign drops in September—look for us as it rolls out in NYC subway cars, on billboards and across digital platforms.
