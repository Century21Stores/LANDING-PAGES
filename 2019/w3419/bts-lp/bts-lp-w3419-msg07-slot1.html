WHO: Jason Rider
WHAT: Freelance Stylist
WHERE: New York City

Starting his career in 2008 as the senior fashion editor of T, The New York Times Style Magazine, Jason is a freelance stylist based in New York. Along with some of the industry’s rising photographers, he’s worked with Bruce Weber, Craig McDean, Inez and Vinoodh, and Collier Schorr.
