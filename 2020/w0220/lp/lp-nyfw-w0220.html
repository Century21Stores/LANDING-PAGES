THE COLOR ENTHUSIAST
ANNIKA WHITE

​
@annikawhite

Out of Cali and on to Brooklyn, this stylist, filmmaker and photographer has opened numerous high-profile doors since her modeling career began at age 12—Stüssy Japan, Hunter Boots, The Times, Cosmopolitan, V and Paper magazines to name a few.

Drawn to the eccentric side of fashion, she loves mixing bright colors (pink is her fave) and takes cues from the ’60s and ’70s to bring vintage elements into every look. On her radar this year? Extra colorful faux fur, leather trench coats and knee-high boots.
